Lokoly.com

In the spring of 2019 my friend Nicholas and I created an ecommerce company called Lokoly. Our mission was to create a website that connected local producers to consumers wishing to shop locally produced CPGs. Over the six months of my envolvement we were able to fulfill over 150 orders with 13 vendors registered on the site. My responsibilites included site maintenance, customer UI experience and design, troubleshooting/customer-vendor support, and content updates. You can shop local at Lokoly.com.

RyderScooters

Ryder Scooters was a group project for MIS 407. The goal of Ryder Scooters was to provide a last mile transportation service for college students. We created a simple web app that allowed customers to find out more about Ryder Scooters, create or log into an account, and reserve/checkout a scooter. We also developed a manager dashboard with statistics on scooter usage, and most traveled routes. Ryderscooters.com

ISU Dining Database Application

ISU Dining Database Application was a group project for MIS 320. We were givin the task to create a database appplication for a local business.
